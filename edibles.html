Edibles - The Firecracker

The quickest and easiest edible

Want to make edibles but are intimidated by the time and effort required? Try making firecrackers. The beauty is that you go from fresh bud to in your mouth in less than 30 minutes. No decarboxylating, no straining butter, no mess. Easy and straightforward is what nugnerd is all about.

It's simple!

  • Here's what you'll need (crackers, weed, peanut butter, tin foil)
  • Lay out two crackers
  • Spread peanut butter on both
  • Sprinkle weed on one
  • Close it up and wrap it in tinfoil
  • Bake at 320°F for 23 minutes - it's easy to remember, three twenty for twenty three - see what I did there?
  • Take it out and you're done. Be careful, these are strong. I find they taste pretty bad, so have something to wash it down with
  • You can experiment with other spread like Nutella and Marshmallow Fluff
  • Don't get caught up in the hype, regular ol peanut butter works fine, it doesn't have to be organic or anything.
  • Same goes for crackers, I usually use whatever I have on hand. Wheat thins, Toppables, etc. If I use Bretons I call it a "Great Breton"

Want to be more precise? Try Stash Cap. Cure and store between 55%-60% for best results. Experiment and have fun!
