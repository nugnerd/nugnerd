Storing Cannabis

Storage is important!

But what's wrong with my plastic baggie? Although you can store weed in anything, proper storage will reward you with clean, even burning, flavourful bud! Smoking isn't your thing? We've detailed proper storage for smoking, vaping and even baking!

It's simple!

  • Place buds in a glass jar and tighten the lid.
  • Store in a cool, dark place (inside a drawer)
  • Humidity is important! 55%-65% for smoking, 40-50% for vaping, 30-40% for baking.

Want to be more precise? Try Stash Cap. Cure and store between 55%-60% for best results. Experiment and have fun!
