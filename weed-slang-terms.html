Weed Slang and Terms

A list of terms, explained simply

What does dank mean? What's a dab? Am I a vape god? Check our our comprehensive list below of weed slang and terms all explained!

The List

  • Bong - A device used to smoke cannabis that uses water filtration
  • Cannabis - The flower of a female cannabis plant
  • Concentrate - A concentrated form of cannabis
  • Dab - A method of smoking cannabis concentrates
  • Edible - Cannabis prepared in food
  • Ganja - Cannabis
  • HID - High intensity discharge, a lighting technology
  • Joint - A cannabis cigarette
  • LED - Light emitting diode, a lighting technology
  • Marijuana - Cannabis
  • pH - Power of hydrogen
  • Vape - Short for vaporizer, a device that creates vapour as opposed to combustion.
  • Weed - Cannabis
