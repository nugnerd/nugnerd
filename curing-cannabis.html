Curing Cannabis

Why cure?

After harvesting and drying, curing is the next step. The goal of curing is to further dry the bud so that it burns evenly, while improving taste and smell.

It's simple!

  • Place buds in a glass jar and secure the lid.
  • Remove the lid once per day for 15 minutes.
  • Repeat until satisfied, usually a couple weeks.

Want to be more precise? Try Stash Cap. Cure and store between 55%-60% for best results. Experiment and have fun!
