CureCap

Take the guess work out of curing. Introducing the CureCap.
Made to fit a standard Mason Jar the CureCap allows you to precisely control the humidity of your harvest.
Not only eliminating the risk of ruining your bud but also delivering a more effective cure, increasing potency and flavour!
