Cure Cap

Take the guess work out of curing. Introducing the Cure Cap.
Made to fit a standard Mason Jar the Cure Cap will allow you to precisely control the humidity of your harvest.
Not only eliminating the risk of ruining your bud but also delivering a more effective cure, increasing potency and flavour!
We are seeking beta testers for the Cure Cap. Interested? Sign up now!

Simple

The cure cap fits on to a variety of standard jars, from Mason to Bick's, we've got you covered

Effective

Take the guess work out of curing. Cure cap allows you to choose your desired humidity level and maintains it throughout the cure.

Affordable

Cure Cap will cost less than the product it cures and unlike dessicants, it's resuable.
