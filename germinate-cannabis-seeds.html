Germinating cannabis seeds

How to germinate cannabis seeds

Here, we go over how to germinate seeds. This method is not only the easiest, but provides the highest success rate!

It's simple!

  • Here's what you'll need (small plate, paper towel, seeds, water)
  • Fold the paper in half twice
  • Place the paper towel on the plate and pour water on it until it is fully saturated
  • Open the paper towel and place your seeds inside, then close it back up
  • Make sure the paper towel doesn't dry out. If the seeds dry out, you're gonna have a bad time
  • Don't seal the paper towel in a plastic baggie, the seeds need oxygen to germiante
  • Keep it in a warm place and out of direct sunlight. Room temperature is fine (21 °C or 70 °F) slightly warmer is even better.
  • The seeds should all "pop" around 7 days, some earlier, some later. Now you're ready to plant!
  • The sprout coming out of seed is a root and it should be planted in the soil, with the seed end popping out of the soil. Coffee cups work great for this
  • Put the seedling in a sunny window (South facing is ideal, but East or West will work as well), mist it with a spray bottle and watch it grow!
