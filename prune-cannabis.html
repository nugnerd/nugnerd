Pruning cannabis plants

Pruning doesn't have to be scary

After putting it all that hard work, you might be scared to chop up your plant. Fimming, topping, lollipopping - but what do they all mean?

It's simple!

  • Here's what you'll need (your favorite pruners)
  • Clean your pruners up, no point in risking killing your plant from dirty shears
  • Start by removing any dead or brown leaves
  • Remove a few of the lower branches. These buds will be small and a waste of time in the end
  • Remove the bud growth up to the tip of each branch, this way the plants puts all its energy into the big buds at the ends of the branches.
  • Some poeple like to do extreme training like LST and Lollipopping. I like to let the plant do its thing with a bit of guidance. You will find your own style
  • Remember, different strains like to be pruned differently, experiment and see what works best for you
