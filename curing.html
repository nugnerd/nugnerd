Curing 101

A quick and simple guide to curing

Why cure?

After you've harvested your plant and hung it to dry, curing is the next step. The point of curing is to further dry the bud, while elevating its taste and aroma.

It's simple!

Curing is a very simple process. Follow the steps below.

  • Place buds in a glass jar and secure the lid.
  • Remove the lid once per day for 15 minutes.
  • That's it

Were you expecting more? Curing is often overcomplicated and more detailed than it needs to be. While it's true you can be more precise and obtain better results, for the majority of homegrowers it's just not worth the time and effort.
